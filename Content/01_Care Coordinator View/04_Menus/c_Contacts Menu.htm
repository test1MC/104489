Contacts Menu

The Contact Main Screen menu has a view sort option dropdown which determines what the main screen will show.

All Contact: Lists all contacts throughout the SalesForce software. This will include staff, patients, patient family members, business partners, etc. Anyone who has been entered into the system will be displayed here.

Birthdays This Month: Lists all contacts having birthdays this month. This will include staff, patients, patient family members, business partners, etc. Anyone who has a birthday this month and has been entered into the system will be displayed here.

My Contacts: Will list only contacts your UserID has designated as contacts.

FormFast Community Profile Dropdown

The edit icon indicates all of these fields are editable. Click the icon next to each option to open the fields for editing.

Preferred Contact Method: This is the method by which the Contact has elected to receive information. Available options are: email, phone and text.

No Emails: If this box is selected, the Contact has opted out of receiving emails.

Preferred Contact Time: This dropdown indicates a preferred contact time. Available options are "morning" or "afternoon".

No Text: If this box is select, the Contact has opted out of receiving texts.
