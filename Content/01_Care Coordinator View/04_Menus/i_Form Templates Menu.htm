Form Templates Menu

The Form Templates Menu has a view sort option dropdown which determines what the main screen will show.

A user can sort their form templates list by selecting one of the below:

All: to view ALL Form Templates in the system

Recently Viewed: Narrows the displayed list of templates to only those most recently viewed

Once the desired list of templates is showing, several options are available to manage the list:

 

 

Use of this menu is described in detail below.

New, Import, List View Controls, Refresh, Edit List, Show Charts and Show Filters. These options are all detailed in the Common Features section.
