Working with Checklists

A Checklist is a collection of Care Steps. Checklists are assigned to patients for them to complete to ensure a successful Care Event.

A Pre-Care Checklist should be completed prior to the patient's Care Event.
A Post-Care Checklist should be completed after the patient's Care Event.

This designation occurs when creating a Checklist. The number of days due, either before or after the Care Event, is also set when creating the Checklist, using the threshold field.
