Working with the Care Step Library

Your organization can build up a library of Care Steps for reuse. Over time, your library will consist of many Care Steps which can be used repeatedly across various patient Checklists.

Once you add a Care Step, it's added to your library permanently. For example, adding the self-care Care Step, "Stop Fluids at Midnight" can be created once and added to multiple Checklists across as many Care Events as needed.

Think of Care Steps as items on your patient's to-do list. You can assign those items over and over to different patients, having different procedures. Building up a library of Care Steps will make creating Checklists faster and easier for anyone coordinating patient care.
