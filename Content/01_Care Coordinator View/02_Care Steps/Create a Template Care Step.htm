Care Steps can be re-used across checklists. One example is a the Self-Care Step of Fasting. Your organization may choose to create one Care Step called "Fasting," and have it available to your Coordinators when creating Checklists.

For ease-of-use, consistency and analytical purposes, we recommend creating Care Steps as templates, and re-using them across your patient Checklists. This will save time as your Coordinators create Checklists for their Patients.

 

To View the existing Care Step Templates, navigate to the Care Step tab, and select the "All Template Steps" from the View menu. Don't forget to press Go!

The list of Care Step Templates will appear for your review.

 

Add a New Care Step

To add a new Care Step Template, select New Care Step.

Select the Record Type of "Template."

 

Enter the following Care Step Template Data:

  1. Care Step Name
  2. Type
  3. Due Date
  4. Due Threshold
  5. Form Template
  6. Form
  7. Care Checklist
  8. Status

SCREENSHOT?

Once you add your Care Step, you can select Back to List: Care Steps to see it in your list of Care Step Templates.
