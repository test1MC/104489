Working with Care Steps

What is a Care Step?

A care step is a single task for a patient to perform.

There are three types of steps:

  1. Form
  2. Diagnostic
  3. Self-care

 

Form Steps: 

Form Steps are associated with a form the patient is expected to complete. For example, a Form Step might be to "Sign a Consent Form". The structured data from the form will be captured and stored in Salesforce while an image of the completed form will be stored as a pdf in Salesforce.

Diagnostic Steps:

A Diagnostic Step represents a test the patient must schedule and complete. For example: use a Diagnostic Step when the patient should schedule lab work and go to a lab to get it done.

Self-Care Steps:

A Self-Care Step represents a step the patient must complete on their own. Examples of Self-Care steps include: fasting, stopping medication and wound care.
