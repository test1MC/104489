Care Coordinator's Main Screen

The Care Coordinator's Main Screen offers them the option to view ALL Patients or Only My Patients.

Selecting the Only My Patients filter will narrow the scope of the patient list.

 
A search can also be conducted to find a specific patient.

The Care Coordinator can further limit the returned results by selecting one of the timing filters.

Selecting Overdue limits the patient list to just those patients who have an overdue care event waiting further action.
Selecting Due limits the returned patient list to only those who currently have a care event due.
The Current list will return only patients whose care events are not yet due.

To the right of the coordinator screen is provided patient information indicated by the previously selected search and filter criteria.

Patient Details includes the selected patient's Date of Birth, Preferred Contact Method, Email address, Phone number and Mailing Address
Tasks includes a list of tasks the patient must complete in order to be current.

The Patient List provides a list of all of the patients meeting the criteria selected above. It also provides the following:

The patient contact card provides patient-specific information relative to this patient.

The patient contact icons allows the coordinator to select a contact method, view which contact method is the preferred patient contact method and sends an automated reminder to the patient to review and complete their care events checklist.

 
The care events checklist may have one or multiple checklist events.

The status column shows if an event is Overdue, Due, or Current and the date of the status.
