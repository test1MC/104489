SalesForce Org Post-Install Community Setup

Communities are spaces for employees, customers and partners to collaborate on best practices

1. Go to SETUP > App Setup > Customize > Communities > Community Settings

If blank, click to enable communities

2.Select a domain name that represents your company. E.g., MyHospital.force.com

Click SAVE. Verify communities are now enabled.

3. Click New Community.

4.Choose a template. Select the “Salesforce Tabs + VisualForce” template.

5.Name your community. Suggestion: “Patient Community”.

Click "Create Community".

6. Click “Go To Community Management” to continue.

7. Navigate to Administration > Members.

Under “Select Permission Sets” click ADD for “FormFast User”.

Click SAVE.

Navigate to Administration > Settings.

Click “Activate Community” and then OK to the pop-up box.
