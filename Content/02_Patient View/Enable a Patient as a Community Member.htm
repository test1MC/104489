Enable a Patient as a Community Member

Enabling a patient as a Community Member allows them access to their patient application, including care checklists.

From the details tab:

Required Fields:

1. Click Manage External User

2. Select Enable Customer User

3. Confirm email and username

4. In the User License field, select Customer Community

5. In the Profile field, select FormFast Patient Community User

 

6. Accept all other defaults

7. Verify "Generate New Password and Notify user Immediately" is checked.

8. Click Save.
