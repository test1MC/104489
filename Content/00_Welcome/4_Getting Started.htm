Getting Started

Once you log in to FormFast Connect your default main screen will typically be a Patient Status screen sorted to view

Using the FormFast Connect Main Screen

The FormFast Connect Main Screen has many options that are shared throughout the various Connect menus. For information about using Common Features, please see that section.

This page can be sorted with the Patient Timeline Status indicators.

Patient Timeline Status

The Patient Timeline Status identifies and filters patients according to where they fall in the care process: Overdue, Due or Current.

Information about setting a Timeline can be found HERE: create cross-reference

Overdue Field

The Overdue Field indicates a Patient has failed to complete Checklist or Care actions within the time frame allotted to complete those actions. These patient actions will be indicated in RED.

 
Due: Care and Checklist items that fall within the Due period are listed under this heading. Any Care or Checklist item that is currently due will be indicated in yellow.
Current: Patients who don't currently have any Care or Checklist items due will be listed under the current tab. Their color indicator will be purple.

This main screen can also be sorted to view All Patients or Only My Patients.

Patient Sort

All Patients: The Home screen can be sorted to display all the patients listed in the system by clicking this button.

 

Only My Patients: The Home screen can also be sorted to display only patients whose XXXX indicator is associated with my UserID.

From this main screen, I can select a patient and more information about them will become available in the Patient Details and Tasks section.

Patient Details Box

Patient Details: The Patient Details tab provides information about the selected patient: Date of Birth, the preferred method of contact (either email, phone or text), the patient's primary phone number, their mailing address and any tasks with today as the due date.

Information on setting these details can be found XXXXX (set cross reference)

 

Tasks: The tasks tab opens existing tasks for review and further action.

 

The SEND REMINDER button will send an automatic email, SMS text or phone message (depending on the selected "Preferred" method of contact) to the patient reminding them of the Care Steps or Care Events that are currently outstanding.

In this example, the preferred method of contact is an email.
